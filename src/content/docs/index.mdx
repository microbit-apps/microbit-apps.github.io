Explore engaging micro:bit apps designed to enhance learning and creativity. 
Micro:bit apps run on the [BBC micro:bit](/getting-started/intro/) and make use of 
[display shields](/apps/display-shield/) for visual output and additional inputs.
**Note: micro:bit apps require micro:bit v2.**

## Featured apps

- [MicroCode](/apps/microcode/) - a visual programming environment for beginners
- [MicroData](/apps/microdata/) - Collect and visualize sensor data from your micro:bit

## MakeCode extensions

- [Display Shield Extension](/extensions/display-shield-ext/) - Add support for display shield in MakeCode for the micro:bit

## Get Involved

Join our community and contribute to the growing ecosystem of micro:bit applications.
