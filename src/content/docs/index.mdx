Explore engaging micro:bit apps designed to enhance learning and creativity. 
Micro:bit apps run on the BBC micro:bit and make use of a display
shield for visual output and additional inputs.

## Featured apps

- [MicroCode](/apps/microcode/) - A visual programming environment for beginners
- [MicroData](/apps/microdata/) - Collect and visualize sensor data from your micro:bit

## MakeCode extensions

- [Display Shield Extension](/extensions/display-shield/) - Add support for display shield in MakeCode for the micro:bit

## Get Involved

Join our community and contribute to the growing ecosystem of micro:bit applications. Whether you're a student, educator, or maker, there's something for everyone.
